How to Play

In this game, you must guess a hidden number within a certain range.

  • Select a difficulty level: Easy (1–10), Medium (1–100), or Hard (1–1000)
  • Type your guess and hit Guess
  • You will be told if your guess is too high or too low
  • Keep guessing until you find the correct number
  • Your score (number of attempts) will be recorded on the scoreboard

Good luck and have fun!
